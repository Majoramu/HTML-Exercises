Quantum Well

Square infinite well

Published 08-13-2025

A square infinite well, also known as an infinite potential well or a particle in a box, is a quantum mechanical model describing a particle confined to a region with impenetrable barriers. The potential is zero inside the well and infinite outside, meaning the particle is trapped and cannot exist beyond the defined boundaries.
